A DashClock extension that will count the days, hours, and minutes until an event.
<b>You will need DashClock Widget by Roman Nurik to use this extension.</b>

You can see the source for this app on GitHub: http://goo.gl/ILO9R9

This app was inspired by I/O 2013 DashClock Extension by NPike: http://goo.gl/1o1id

If the countdown is not appearing on your DashClock, please try re-installing DashClock Widget. If the problem still exists, send an email to support@cr5315.com with your device name, Android version, if it's rooted, and any other information you wish to provide. Thanks!
